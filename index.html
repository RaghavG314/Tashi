Our Story

A Family Passion
for Authentic Craft

Tashi was born from a simple idea: that Australians who love Indian fashion deserve access to genuinely authentic garments — not mass-produced copies, but the real thing.

"My niece sources every single piece from the finest markets and artisans in India, so you can be confident you're getting something truly special."

Based in Melbourne, we bring you a rotating collection of women's, men's, and children's Indian wear — all handpicked, all authentic, all with a story behind them.
